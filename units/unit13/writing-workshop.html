Step 1: Planning – Think First!

🏠 Topic sentence = Foundation 🧱 Supporting details = Walls 🏁 Conclusion = Roof
📌 Model Paragraph:
Schools are important places for learning. Students go to school to study many subjects like math, science, and languages. Classrooms are welcoming and filled with interesting things to see. Teachers are friendly and help students understand new things. They encourage us to ask questions and participate in discussions. Many schools have special events, like sports days and cultural celebrations. Students enjoy working together in groups and sharing their talents. In conclusion, schools are places where students learn and make friends. They help us grow and prepare for the future. I am happy to be a part of my school community.
